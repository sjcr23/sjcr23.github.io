ITCR Biodiversidad

Teaching Data Science for biodiversity conservation.

This is an example post with advanced image components.

ITCR Biodiversidad is a research and extension group of Instituto Tecnológico de Costa Rica (ITCR), with an emphasis on biodiversity conservation. Created in 2021 by Professor María Auxiliadora Mora Cross and a group of female computer science students in order to contribute to biodiversity conservation.

Python Classes

Part of my work in this project has been to collaborate as a teacher. We gave a course on Introduction to Python for Data Science to female students of the Universidad Técnica Nacional (UTN) where I was able to share my knowledge learned in the area, as well as learn new skills and experiences in teaching.

I had the opportunity to teach on the following topics:

Lesson (Spanish) Google Colabs
Python Fundamentals Python basics
Introduction to OOP and Pandas POO & Pandas
NumPy, vectores y matrices NumPy
Geographic information systems (GIS) GIS - GeoPandas

Feel free to visit the "Teaching" tab where I am gradually posting the course materials viewed.

CLEI 2024

Currently, I am co-developing a paper on the work we did with the UTN girls, which seeks to communicate about the perspective of university students on research in Data Science, biodiversity conservation, and gender gaps in computing.

This in order to present our work at the 50a Conferencia Latinoamericana de Informática (CLEI), which celebrates its fiftieth edition in Bahía Blanca, Argentina. The work carried out as a whole is still under competition, it has not yet been approved by the CLEI and has not yet been published.
